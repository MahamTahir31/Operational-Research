SERVICES WE PROVIDE

You can access each customer's details, including their names, day of service, type of service, counter they were served by, and other information, from our three days' worth of records of customers.

Four counters make up our bank, and each counter has a lead who deals with customers. Depending on the nature of their concerns, customers can find answers at any counter.

The primary function of this website is the ability to simulate customer information, queueing parameters, and analyse based customers on their arrival, service, turnaround, wait, and response times.
